About EduPredict

EduPredict AI leverages advanced Machine Learning models to help students understand their academic potential. By analyzing study patterns, sleep habits, and previous results, we provide actionable insights.

Our Technology:

  • Linear Regression: Predicts exact percentage scores.
  • Logistic Regression: Classifies high-performance status.
  • Secure DB: Encrypted user session management via SQLite3.
